# Universe & Characters Spec

## 1. The Tone
A humorous but highly stressful mix of college stereotypes and genuine reality. The game contrasts bright, friendly UI with harsh life consequences like going bankrupt or missing final exams.

## 2. Onboarding (First Play Flow)
We do not use hand-holding tooltips or a fake tutorial week. 
**The Hook:** The moment a new player begins, they are shown a narrative UI element — an "In-Universe" Email or Phone Text from an older sibling or Dean. This perfectly contextualizes why they have 4 stats to monitor and what University expects of them before throwing them into the deep end.

## 3. Character Setup & Archetypes
- **Initial Rollout:** The player uses a visual avatar creator but acts functionally as a "Blank Slate" starting at 50/100 stats.
- **Future Integration:** **The Archetype System**. Players will select backgrounds like "The Commuter" (Starts with less time, more money) or "The Party Animal" (High social, terrible grades). These inject archetype-exclusive dilemmas into the event engine and permanently modify baseline starting stats to increase replayability.
